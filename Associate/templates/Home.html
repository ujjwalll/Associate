Welcome to Jamm

Get Stuff Done!

Shortcuts

Access everything with Jamm Commands and keyboard shortcuts. Jamm is designed to work with whatever workflows you might have within your team.

Supercharge your workflows

Nobody needs yet another workflow. With Jamm’s Slack integration, slash commands, get more stuff done by having conversations where you already work.

Instant, shareable web links

Invite anyone, join on any device

Use “Jamm Now” to create video jamm huddles and share links with anyone. You can join from the browser or using the native application with a single click. Better yet, all rooms are locked by default and guests have to request access to enter the video huddle.

Huddle with Your Team

Capture the Moment

When you’re remote, casual conversations become formal events with calendar invites and meeting URLs. Jamm helps you capture the moment by making it easy to have short bursty conversations. Chat for a few minutes whenever, it doesn’t have to be an hour long “meeting”.

Free Yourself from Meetings

Rediscover spontaneous conversations

Working remotely doesn’t have to be isolating or lonely. With Jamm’s 1-click voice and video collaboration, coworkers can recreate those casual breakthrough convos that happen when passing someone in the hallway. 

Stay Connected with Your Team

Lightweight and out of the way

Jamm offers lightweight collaboration features you need – voice, video, recording, screen-sharing, white-boarding, shared pointers, and more – all in one single click. 

Built for Different Time Zones

Consume at your own pace

Work across different time zones? Jamm makes it easy to send expressive video stories or create video podcasts to let your team know about the cool new project you’re working on. Better yet, capture video snippets of convos to keep others in the loop.

FAQ

Do I need Jamm if we already use zoom/slack?

More than 90% of our customers use Zoom or Hangouts. Jamm is about what happens in-between meetings for spontaneous conversations, feeling connected and lightweight brainstorming.

How do I use Jamm in my workflow?

Join spontaneous conversations, summon people on the fly or do video podcasts to your team. To see how other teams use Jamm, check out this guide.

What are the pricing plans?

In response to current WFH situation as part of the unfolding Covid-19 situations, we are offering Jamm free for the next 3 months for teams.

Why call it Jamm?

We think awesome teams are like rockstar bands. Where everyone plays their part, they come together and jam collaboratively to create something fun and amazing!

What problems do you solve?

Jamm enables lightweight, spontaneous collaboration for staying connected, having informal jam sessions and expressing yourself through audio and video. 

What platforms do you support?

We currently support desktop applications for Mac and Windows. You can also join through the web using links. Linux support coming soon.
